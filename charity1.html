Feeding America one Person at a Time

"Our mission is to feed America's hungry through a nationwide network of member food banks and engage our country in the fight to end hunger."

A little information:

  • Our History

    What began in 1979 as a clearinghouse for national food donations is now the nation’s largest domestic hunger-relief organization—a powerful and efficient network of 200 food banks across the country.

  • Our Leadership

    The men and women who lead Feeding America on the board of directors and our executive team are dedicated individuals from a variety of backgrounds – all dedicated to creating a hunger-free America.

  • Why Feeding America?

    Feeding America is proud to be rated so highly by esteemed watchdog organizations like the Better Business Bureau and Charity Navigator.
